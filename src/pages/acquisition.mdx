# User Acquisition

Explanation of every metric in the User Acquisition section.

- **Total Unique Joins:** The total number of unique users who joined any of your servers (if a user joins multiple servers, they will only be counted once).
- **Unique Joins Today:** The total number of unique users who joined any of your servers today.
- **Unique Joins This Week:** The total number of unique users who joined any of your servers this week.
- **Unique Joins This Month:** The total number of unique users who joined any of your servers this month.
- **Joins Per Day Chart:** A chart showing the number of joins per day over the last 90 days.
This is incredibly insightful as it shows how efficient your marketing efforts are.
